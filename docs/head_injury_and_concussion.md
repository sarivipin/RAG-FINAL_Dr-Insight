# Head injury and concussion

## Question
What should I know about head injury and concussion and how to treat a minor head injury?

### Answer
If you do not need to go to hospital, you can usually look after yourself or your child at home.

It's normal to have symptoms for up to 2 weeks. For example, a slight headache or feeling sick.
