# Shin splints

## Question
What is the treatment for shin splints?

### Answer
Your GP will ask about your symptoms and examine your leg. If it's not getting better, they may advise you to attend a physiotherapist.

## Question
What causes shin splints?

### Answer
Shin splints happen when you put too much stress on your leg. They usually happen when you do exercise like running.

You are more likely to get shin splints if you:

- have started exercising after not being active for some time
- run or jump on hard surfaces
- do not have a good running technique
