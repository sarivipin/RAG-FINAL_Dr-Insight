# Sore or dry lips

## Question
What should I know about sore or dry lips and a pharmacist can help with sore or dry lips?

### Answer
A pharmacist will tell you about the best treatments for dry or sore lips and if you need to see your GP.

You can buy lip balm containing petroleum jelly or beeswax from a pharmacist.

## Question
What should I know about sore or dry lips and common causes of sore or dry lips?

### Answer
Sore or dry lips can be caused by many things.

Common causes include:

- cold weather, dry air or wind
- damage from the sun
- dehydration
- a lip injury
- allergies

Sometimes they can be caused by a lack of vitamins and minerals.

If you have a small, fluid-filled blister on your lip, you may have a cold sore.

Most people with cold sores get a tingling, burning or itching feeling on their lip before the blister appears.
