What causes toe pain?

### Answer
Common causes of a sore toe are exercising too much or wearing shoes that are too tight.

## Question
What should I know about toe pain and a pharmacist can help with toe pain?

### Answer
If you have toe pain, a pharmacist can advise you about:

- the best painkiller to take
- insoles and pads for your shoes
- treatments for common skin and nail problems
- if you need to visit a GP
